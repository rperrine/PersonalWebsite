Career Page

The career I selected is a chef and head cook. To become a chef or head cook you are required to have a high school diploma or equivalent. The average salary is $48,460 per year. The most 3 important job duties for this career are to Check the freshness of food and ingredients, Inspect supplies, equipment, and work areas for cleanliness and functionality, and to order and maintain an inventory of food and supplies. The job outlook is 11% (much faster than average). Some similar occupations are Bakers, Food preparation Workers, and food service managers. to become a baker you do not need any formal education credential. To become a food preparation worker you also do not need any formal education credential. To become a food service manager you are required to have a high school diploma or equivalent.
