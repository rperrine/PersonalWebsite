Our Careers

Here are some of the jobs we offer:

Architecture

Our architects have an average pay of 79,000 dollars. Besides that, they graduated with a Bachelor's degree. Our architects design the layouts of our buildings and work with our clients to get the office buildings, homes, and etc. exactly the way they want them.

Engineering

Our Engineers work with our architects to plan out most of the buildings. They mostly are paid an average of 52,000 dollars. Our engineers also have graduated with Bachelor's degrees.

Writers

We hire writers for the local newspaper. We look for writers that have graduated with Bachelor's degrees. They usually are paid in an average of 62,000 per year. Writers write the content and convert the information that we get from our media teams to what locals read in their newspapers.

Media

Our media teams are composed of reporters, camera man, broadcasters, etc. Our media team memebers are required to have graduated with Bachelor's degrees. They are paid on an average of 43,000 dollars.
